What is OS ZERO?

OS ZERO is more than an operating system. It’s a design philosophy—built for signal, not noise. No infinite scroll. No push distractions. It’s minimal by intention, not trend.

Core Principles

  • • Function over flash
  • • Clarity over convenience
  • • Depth over dopamine
  • • Local-first, open-source by default
  • • You control it — not the other way around
